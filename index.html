Common Questions

What if it rains on my service day?

We work rain or shine! Muddy paws mean muddy yards, and we're equipped for it. Severe storms? We'll reschedule within 24 hours.

Do I need to be home?

Nope! Most customers give us gate access. We'll text when we arrive and when we're done, with photos for peace of mind.

What about my dog's favorite spots?

We know dogs have patterns! After a few visits, we learn your pup's preferences and check those spots extra carefully.

How do you dispose of the waste?

Everything is double-bagged and taken with us to a certified disposal facility. We never use your trash cans unless requested.

Can I skip a week for vacation?

Absolutely! Just text us 24 hours ahead. No charge for skips, and we'll add an extra scoop when you're back!
